Create - Design - Use

André Kaplan is a usability-obsessed tech geek who's into creative photography, great design and mind expanding experiences. He believes in the concept that the user is central in every step and that a service should be designed according to the possibilities and limitations presented by its users.

You can usually find him optimizing a workflow, designing an interface or just taping two things together. He enjoys spending time with his wife, tech, gaming, listening to and making music, photography, bearded dragons and of course Edgar the English Bullterrier.

To get to know him better you can hit him up on the various social media sites or drop him a note to his Gmail.

Native of Norrtälje north of Stockholm, he currently resides with his lovely wife and crazy dog in Lambohov on the outskirts of Linköping, Sweden.
